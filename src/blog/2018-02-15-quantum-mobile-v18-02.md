We've just released version 18.02.0 of the **Quantum Mobile** Virtual Machine. Quantum Mobile now supports running **AiiDA-powered Jupyter Apps** (AiiDAlab) inside Quantum Mobile, just like running them on [aiidalab.materialscloud.org](http://aiidalab.materialscloud.org).

All details and download instructions can be found on the [release page](https://github.com/marvel-nccr/quantum-mobile/releases).
